# For customers

*Audience: business owners, developers, agencies, and anyone who needs hosting or domains directly from Talksasa (or from a reseller’s branded portal).*

## What you get

Talksasa Cloud gives you a modern customer portal to buy, manage, and pay for everything your online presence needs — without tickets and phone calls for routine tasks.

### Web hosting (shared hosting)

- **DirectAdmin-powered shared hosting** tied to your domain
- Automatic provisioning after payment — credentials delivered by email
- Log in to your control panel at **your domain on port 2222**
- Built-in hosting tools: DNS, email accounts, databases, subdomains, FTP, SSL (Let’s Encrypt), cron jobs, and backups
- Optional in-portal hosting dashboard for everyday tasks without leaving Talksasa

**SEO angle:** *Shared web hosting Kenya · DirectAdmin hosting · cPanel alternative Kenya*

### Domain names

- Register and renew **.co.ke, .com, .org**, and other extensions
- Search availability, add domains to cart, and check out with hosting in one order
- Manage **nameservers and DNS records** from your account
- **Transfer domains in** with EPP/auth code support
- Renewal reminders and invoices generated automatically
- Link domains to hosting during checkout (register new, use existing, or transfer)

**SEO angle:** *Buy domain Kenya · .co.ke registration · domain transfer Kenya*

### Cloud app hosting (container hosting)

Deploy modern applications without managing a server:

- Choose your **tech stack** — Laravel, Node.js, Python, and more
- Select runtime versions and databases at checkout
- **Automatic container deployment** after payment
- Manage your app from the portal:
  - Start, stop, restart, redeploy
  - View logs, metrics, and health status
  - **Web terminal** and file manager
  - Bind custom domains and SSL
  - Git repository deploy and Laravel project setup helpers
  - Database query and import tools
  - Scheduled backups

**SEO angle:** *Laravel hosting Kenya · deploy Node.js app · managed cloud hosting*

### VPS & dedicated servers

- Order **VPS and dedicated server** plans for full root access
- Credentials delivered securely after provisioning
- Ideal for custom stacks, high-traffic sites, and enterprise workloads

### Billing & payments

- Clear **invoices with line-item detail** (including which domain a hosting plan serves)
- Download **PDF invoices** for your records
- Pay with methods that work in Kenya and internationally:
  - **M-Pesa** (STK push to your phone)
  - **Stripe** (cards)
  - **PayPal**
  - Bank transfer and manual payment (with admin verification)
- **Account wallet / credits** — top up and apply balance to invoices
- Automatic renewal invoices before services expire
- Payment history and receipt tracking

**SEO angle:** *Pay hosting with M-Pesa · hosting invoice Kenya*

### Account & support

- Secure registration with **email verification** and optional **SMS two-factor authentication**
- Profile management, dark mode, and mobile-friendly portal
- **Support tickets** — open, reply, and track issues in one thread
- Service dashboard: active, pending, suspended, and renewal dates at a glance
- Upgrade paths and service renewal from the portal

# For resellers

*Audience: web designers, IT consultants, agencies, and entrepreneurs who want to sell hosting under their own brand.*

## What Talksasa Cloud does for resellers

Run a **white-label hosting business** without building billing software, payment integrations, or provisioning systems from scratch. Talksasa Cloud is the engine; you are the brand your customers see.

### Your brand, your customers

- **Custom branding** — company name, logo, colors, and optional **custom domain** for your customer portal
- **SSL for your branded domain** — automated certificate provisioning for your reseller site
- Customers sign up and pay **you**; invoices and emails carry **your** identity when configured
- **Impersonate customers** to troubleshoot without sharing passwords

**SEO angle:** *White label hosting reseller · start hosting company Kenya · reseller hosting platform*

### Full customer lifecycle management

- Create and manage **unlimited customers** (within your package limits)
- Order **hosting, domains, and services** on behalf of customers
- Issue **custom invoices**, record payments, resend PDFs, mark paid, or cancel
- **Suspend, unsuspend, and terminate** services from your dashboard
- Welcome emails and notifications via **your own SMTP and SMS** settings

### Product catalog & pricing

- Build a **retail catalog** from platform products (shared hosting, containers, servers)
- Map hosting plans to **DirectAdmin packages** on your assigned infrastructure
- Set **your own retail prices** — you control margin on every sale
- Customers browse **your catalog** inside your branded portal

### Domain reseller program

- **Wholesale domain pricing** — buy at bulk rates, sell at retail
- Set **per-TLD retail prices** (.co.ke, .com, etc.)
- Register domains for customers; orders queue until your **wallet is funded**
- **Auto-push** when wallet balance is sufficient, or push manually
- Manage customer domains: nameservers, DNS, renewals, transfers
- **Transfer domains between your customers** with SMS approval workflow
- Margin visibility in reports

### Payments & wallet

- Connect **your own M-Pesa** credentials — customer payments go to your till
- Configure **SMTP** for branded email and **SMS** for alerts and OTP
- **Reseller wallet** for wholesale domain float — top up via M-Pesa, track every transaction
- Low-balance alerts so domain orders never stall unexpectedly
- Accept customer payments on invoices; platform handles ledger and settlement logic

### Hosting operations

- Provision **shared hosting (DirectAdmin)** and **container apps** for customers
- Monitor service status, disk usage, and enforcement reasons
- Package-based **service limits** — scale your plan as you grow
- Disk quota and overdue-invoice enforcement keeps your infrastructure healthy

### Reports & analytics

- Export **customers, invoices, revenue, services, and margins**
- Dashboard with action queue for overdue subscriptions, low wallet, and pending tasks
- Understand what makes money and what needs attention

### Your own subscription

- Resellers subscribe to a **Talksasa package** (service slots, features, billing cycle)
- Pay your platform subscription via the same invoice and payment flow
- Clear status when renewal is due or account is suspended

# FAQ (SEO-friendly)

### What is Talksasa Cloud?
Talksasa Cloud is a hosting and billing platform that lets individuals and businesses order web hosting, domains, and cloud applications online — and lets hosting resellers run a branded business on the same infrastructure.

### Can I pay with M-Pesa?
Yes. Customers and resellers can pay invoices using M-Pesa STK push, alongside card (Stripe), PayPal, and bank transfer options.

### Do you offer .co.ke domains?
Yes. The platform supports Kenyan and international domain extensions with registration, renewal, and transfer workflows.

### What is white-label reseller hosting?
You sell hosting under your own company name and domain. Talksasa Cloud handles provisioning, billing automation, and infrastructure; you set prices and manage customers.

### How fast is provisioning?
Shared hosting and container services are provisioned automatically after successful payment. Domain registration depends on registry processing once the order is submitted.

### Can I host Laravel or Node.js applications?
Yes. Container hosting supports multiple languages and frameworks with deployment, logs, terminal access, Git integration, and custom domains.

### Is there a control panel for shared hosting?
Yes. Shared hosting uses DirectAdmin. Customers receive login details and can access the panel at their domain on port 2222, or use simplified tools inside the Talksasa customer portal.

### Who is Talksasa Cloud for?
- **Customers** who want hosting, domains, and apps with local payment options  
- **Resellers** who want to start or scale a hosting business without building custom software

# Tone & messaging guidelines

- **Lead with outcomes:** “Get online fast,” “Run your hosting brand,” not “multi-tenant Laravel billing.”
- **Local relevance:** Mention M-Pesa, KES, .co.ke, and Kenya where natural — it differentiates from generic global hosts.
- **Split CTAs:** “Start as a customer” vs “Become a reseller” — never blend the two on one button.
- **Trust:** Automated provisioning, clear invoices, secure payments, ticket support.
- **Reseller pitch:** Margin, brand, wallet, catalog — they are building a *business*, not buying hosting for one site.
